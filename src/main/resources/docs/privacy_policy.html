Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2026

Welcome to Vince Batecan State University (VBSU). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Educational Management System.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized educational experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification: Name, Student ID, and Email address.
  • Academic Data: Course enrollments, grades, subject difficulty ratings, and academic history.
  • Productivity Metrics: Study habits, time management data, and productivity scores tracked through our modeling system.
  • Interaction Data: Logs of your conversations with the AI Chat assistant and career roadmap queries.
  • Reservation & Request Data: Details of documents requested and facility/resource reservations made through the system.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • To generate personalized Career Roadmaps and academic suggestions.
  • To analyze Subject Difficulty and provide tailored study strategies.
  • To facilitate and track Document Requests and Reservations.
  • To improve the AI Chat's accuracy and relevance to your specific curriculum.
  • To model productivity and offer insights into your learning patterns.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

VBSU does not sell your personal data. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Faculty/Advisors: To assist in academic counseling and support.
  • Service Providers: For backend services like database management or AI processing (anonymized where possible).
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. This includes encrypted communications and secure database protocols. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or storage method is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to institutional record-keeping requirements).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the application.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Group 5 Development Team at Vince Batecan State University.
